Everyone this is a procedure on how to make pounded yam

Things you need to get ready before preparing your pounded yam

  • Knief
  • Yam
  • Water
  • Pot
  • Cooking Gas
  • Pounder
  • Blender
  1. Knief

    2. You'll need a knief to help pill the yam and cut into smaller portion for quick cooking

  2. Yam

    3. Pill and cut the yam into smaller portion to aid quick cooking and achieving a soft and easy to consume result afther cooking

  3. Water

    4. You add water to the cooking pot and then place it on the cooking gas to boil the yam and check back later to make sure the yam is well cooked and soft

  4. Pot

    5. The pot serves as a contianer which is used to cook the yam till it get done

  5. Cooking gas

    6. The cooking gas helps makes the fire and have our yam cooked

  6. Pounder

    7. This is a very important item needed to get our result. The pounder is a wooden contianer where yams and any other poundable content are place for proper merching/poundering till the content in it finally becomes what we want them to be, into other words the wooden pounder is an accient item basically used for making a pounded yam which the African also call (iyan) the end product and ready to be served as our meal

  7. Blender

    8. The Blender serves as an alternative method. Likes it was explained, this is an advanced method of getting our result (pounded yam) technologically without having to go through the stress of effortless pounding. it also saved us engery and consume lesser time.
